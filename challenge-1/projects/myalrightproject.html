My Favorite Place - My Parent's Farm

I love my parent's home and farm because it has a lot of things that are calming and centering to me. They have a comfortable home, gardens, outbuildings, 20+ acres of generally untouched woods, and some pets. One of my favorite parts of going there is the drive, though. It is about an hour through the city, suburbs, then the countryside so there is time to kind of unwind, see a variety of scenery, and then get some breathing room once you arrive. I loved being in the woods and taking long walks as a child and am coming to realize the power of a walk in the woods again as an adult.

3 of my favorite things about the farm:

  • Laid back
  • Ability to find solitude in the woods
  • Being around family in a comfortable setting
